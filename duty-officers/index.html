Duty Officers

During all days of the conference, including pre-conference and sponsored evening activities, there will be Duty Officers available to help provide support for anyone who been harassed, threatened, abused, or feels the CodeOfConduct4Lib has been violated during the conference. Below is a list of all duty officers, their photos, and the times they will be "on duty".

Contacting a Duty Officer

To contact a duty officer, you can

  • Call or text 678-218-8064.
  • Send an email to c4l17-duty-officers@googlegroups.com. Only Duty Officers are subscribed to this mailing list, and no archive is kept.
  • Approach a Duty Officer in person, pictures of whom are below.
  • Ask a volunteer at the Luskin Center registration desk to summon a Duty Officer.
